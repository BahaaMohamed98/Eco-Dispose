E-Receiver For E-Waste Recycling

This project focuses on the proper collection, recycling, and disposal of electronic waste (e-waste) to minimize environmental impact. It includes awareness campaigns, e-waste collection drives, and partnerships with recycling facilities to ensure safe processing of discarded electronics. The project aims to reduce pollution, recover valuable materials, and promote sustainable practices in e-waste management.

What can I recycle in E-Receiver?

Smart Phones

Smartphones, PDAs that support cellular phones, touch screen technology, and mobile devices.

Computers

Computers and all their components such as motherboards, cables, and internal parts.

Laptops & Tablets

Includes laptops and tablets, along with their parts like screens, CPUs, and other useful components.

How can I recycle my device in E-Receiver?

Sign up

Create an account and fill in your details to place an order.

Share your Device

Provide details and upload photos of your device.

Accept Offer

Receive offers from recycling companies. Accept one, and we will send a delivery person to complete the process.

Why recycling?

Energy Efficiency

Reduces energy consumption compared to producing new materials.

Resource Conservation

Saves natural resources by recovering valuable materials.
